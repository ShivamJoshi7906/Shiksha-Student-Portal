Placement activities at SHIKSHA are built on a foundation of structure, transparency, and integrity. The University follows a well-defined placement policy, a transparent slotting process, and enforces a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding student conduct and discipline during placement drives.

  • Engagement in industry and R&D expert talks
  • Involvement in high-quality events, including workshops and summer/winter schools
